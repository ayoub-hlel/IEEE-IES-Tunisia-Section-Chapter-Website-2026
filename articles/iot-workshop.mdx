**As part of IEEE Education Week**, the **IES Tunisia Chapter** organized an **online workshop** titled *"Introduction to IoT"* (Internet of Things).**This event was held in collaboration with the IAS, RAS ISIMS Student Branches chapters**.**The goal of the workshop was to introduce participants to the fundamentals of IoT**, including its **core concepts, areas of application**, and **development tools**, through an interactive session accessible to beginners.
