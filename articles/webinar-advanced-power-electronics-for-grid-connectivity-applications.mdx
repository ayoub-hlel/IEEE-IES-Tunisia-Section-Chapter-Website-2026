The IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IES) Tunisia Section Chapter hosted a technical session in collaboration with IEEE Libya Subsection on **Advanced Power Electronics for Grid-Connectivity Applications**, presented by **Prof. Hadi Y. Kanaan**. This session provided participants with valuable insights into modern power electronics and their critical role in enhancing grid connectivity.

Prof. Kanaan discussed key advancements, challenges, and applications in the field, offering attendees a deeper understanding of power electronics integration in smart grids. The session was an excellent opportunity for students, researchers, and professionals to expand their knowledge and engage in discussions on the future of grid-connected power systems.
