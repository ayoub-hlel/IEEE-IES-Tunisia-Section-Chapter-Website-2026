To celebrate the IEEE Day, the IEEE IES Tunisia Section Chapter hosted a series of Technical and Non-Technical Workshops designed to empower members with both professional insights and practical skills.

The series kicked off on Friday, October 17th, with a session focused on “IEEE IES Benefits.” Led by Mohamed Rebai—the Student Representative for the IEEE Tunisia Section and Treasurer for the IES Tunisia Chapter—the webinar explored the value of joining the society. As the incoming IEEE IES Ambassador for 2025, Mohamed provided a comprehensive look at how members can maximize their opportunities within the organization.

The celebration continued on Tuesday, October 21st, with a technical deep-dive into “Git/GitHub.” This session was conducted by Med Amin Bouaziz, a Software Engineer at Creativas and the IEEE Tunisia Section Webmaster. Drawing on his experience as an online instructor, Med Amin guided attendees through the essentials of version control and collaboration in software development.

Both sessions were held online via Google Meet, allowing members to connect and learn from the comfort of their homes. A big thank you to our speakers and attendees for making this IEEE Day celebration a success!
