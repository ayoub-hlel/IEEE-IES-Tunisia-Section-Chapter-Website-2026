From December 18th to 20th, 2023, we had the pleasure to represent the IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IES) Tunisia Section Chapter at the 11th Tunisian Students & Young Professionals Congress (TSYP 11) in Medina Center Hammamet. Setting up our booth in the venue’s main hall, we were excited to meet students, young professionals, and IEEE members from all around Tunisia.

Throughout the event, our booth attracted many visitors eager to learn more about our initiatives. We had insightful discussions, shared experiences, and explored potential collaborations with passionate individuals. The enthusiasm and curiosity of the attendees reinforced our commitment to fostering a dynamic and innovative IEEE community in Tunisia.

Being part of TSYP 11 was a rewarding experience, allowing us to connect with like-minded individuals and strengthen our network. We look forward to future events where we can continue promoting industrial electronics and engaging with the next generation of engineers and researchers.
