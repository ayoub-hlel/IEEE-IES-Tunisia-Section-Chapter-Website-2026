On December 29th, the IEEE IES Tunisia Section Chapter gathered at ESPRIT for a special IES Day celebration. We had a great turnout, with Student Members and Young Professionals traveling from all over the country to join us.

The event operated under the theme "Tunisian IES is The New Carthage," and the schedule was packed from late afternoon right through until the next morning. Here is a quick report on what we covered: Evening Sessions: Technical & Soft Skills

We kicked things off at 17:00 with the opening ceremony. After a quick coffee break, we got straight to work at 18:30 with Technical Round Tables, which gave members a chance to discuss current trends in industrial electronics.

After dinner, at 21:00:

A Public Speaking Workshop for members looking to improve their communication skills.

The Overnight Challenge:

The atmosphere shifted late in the evening. At 22:30, we revealed the "IES Day Surprise," which served as the warm-up for the main event.

From midnight until 7:00 AM, attendees participated in "Rise of Carthage: The IES Game." It was an all-night activity that kept everyone engaged and working together in teams until sunrise. Wrapping Up

We finished the marathon event with breakfast at 7:00 AM, followed by the closing ceremony at 8:00 AM. It was a long night, but it was a fantastic opportunity to combine technical discussions with some fun team building.

Thanks to everyone who stayed up with us!
