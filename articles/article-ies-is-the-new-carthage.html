The IEEE IES Tunisia Chapter is expanding its mission with its 2025 flagship campaign, IES is the New Carthage. This program is critical to the chapter’s objective of opening more than 20 student branches by the end of 2025.

The initiative selects committed student leaders, known as Guardians of the Flame, from Student Branches all over Tunisia. Their purpose is to represent the Industrial Electronics Society locally by creating new Student Branch Chapters and promoting IES activities among their peers.

This grassroots technique motivates students by establishing a culture of leadership and ownership. By decentralizing their growth efforts, the IES is the New Carthage program is effectively creating a strong and integrated community of future innovators across the country.
