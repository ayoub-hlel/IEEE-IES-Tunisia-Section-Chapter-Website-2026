The **IEEE IES Tunisia Section Chapter**, in collaboration with the **IEEE SMCS Tunisia Section Chapter** and the **IEEE EdSoc Tunisia Section Chapter**, is proud to host the **12th Edition of the TSYP Technical Challenge**. This year’s theme focuses on developing innovative solutions that merge technology with education, with a spotlight on the **Chess Coach Robot** project.

**The Challenge **

The core objective of the challenge is to design and develop a **Chess Coach Robot** that fosters sustainability, safety, and technological innovation in the education of young students (K-12).
