The IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IES) Tunisia Section Chapter, in collaboration with the IEEE Libya Subsection, organized an insightful online webinar to discuss the latest advancements in industrial electronics and strengthen regional collaboration within IEEE. Hosted via Google Meet, the session was led by **Eng. Aziz Ben Kbaier**, who provided valuable insights into IES activities and opportunities for IEEE members in Tunisia and Libya.

The webinar aimed to introduce participants to the mission and vision of IES, highlight key research areas, and discuss potential collaborations between Tunisia and Libya. Eng. Aziz Ben Kbaier emphasized the importance of knowledge sharing and cross-border cooperation in advancing industrial electronics and its applications.

Attendees had the opportunity to engage in discussions, ask questions, and explore how they could contribute to and benefit from IEEE IES initiatives. The session attracted students, researchers, and professionals eager to enhance their understanding of industrial electronics and connect with experts in the field.
