Participation of the IES Tunisia Chair in a scientific event organized as part of the PEJC project: “A Step Towards Environmental Sustainability – Intelligent Waste Classification”.
The event was initiated by the Electricity Research Center at ENIS, in collaboration with the Department of Electrical Engineering, and gathered research students around innovative environmental topics.
On this occasion, Mohamed Ali Krichen led a session on IoT in the context of Industry 5.0, with a focus on industrial and sustainable applications.
